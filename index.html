Brief Biography 

Mr. Xiaogen Zhou received his B.S. degree from Zhixing College of Northwest Normal University in 2009, and Ph.D degree from Guijun Zhang's Lab at College of Information Engineering in Zhejiang University of Technology in 2018. From 2016.12-2018.2, he acted as a jointly trianed PhD student of China Scholar Council at the University of Michigan (Ann Arbor) in USA. He is currently a Post-Doctoral Fellow of Yang Zhang Lab at Department of Computational Medicine and Bioinformatics in University of Michigan (Ann Arbor). His current research interests include intelligent information processing, optimization theory and algorithm design, and bioinformatics.
